# Financial Model Specification for "My Business" (NutriTrack SaaS)

## I. Key Assumptions

### A. Customer Acquisition
*   **Monthly New Customers:**
    *   Bear Case: 20
    *   Base Case: 25
    *   Bull Case: 30
*   **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**
    *   Bear Case: $150
    *   Base Case: $125
    *   Bull Case: $100

### B. Revenue
*   **Average Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) per Customer (ARPU):**
    *   Bear Case: $18
    *   Base Case: $20
    *   Bull Case: $22
*   **Monthly Churn Rate:**
    *   Bear Case: 8%
    *   Base Case: 5%
    *   Bull Case: 3%

### C. Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
*   **Variable Cost per Customer (e.g., hosting, support):**
    *   Base Case: $2 per customer

### D. Operating Expenses (Monthly)
*   **Sales & Marketing Spend:** $1,500 (fixed)
*   **Product Development (Salaries, Tools):** $3,000 (fixed)
*   **General & Administrative (Admin, Legal, Office):** $1,000 (fixed)

## II. Unit Economics (Monthly)

*   **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)**
*   **ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)**
*   **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**
*   **LTV (Lifetime Value):** ARPU * Gross Margin % / Monthly Churn Rate
*   **LTV:CAC Ratio**
*   **Payback Period:** CAC / Monthly Gross Profit per Customer

## III. Profit & Loss (P&L) - Monthly Projection

*   **Revenue**
*   **COGS**
*   **Gross Profit**
*   **Operating Expenses:**
    *   Sales & Marketing
    *   Product Development
    *   General & Administrative
*   **Net Income**

## IV. Sensitivity Analysis

*   **Impact of +/- 20% change on Net Income for:**
    *   Monthly New Customers
    *   ARPU
    *   Monthly Churn Rate
    *   CAC

## V. Output Format

*   **JSON:** Structured data for model inputs, outputs, and scenarios.
*   **Excel Workbook:** Visual representation of the model, P&L, unit economics, and sensitivity analysis with formulas.
